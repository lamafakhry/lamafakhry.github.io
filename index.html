Eat colorfully!

The colors of fruits and vegetables tell you a lot about what nutrients they contain. Deeper, darker colors are associated with higher antioxident and phytonutrient content. As you shop and cook, aim for a range of colors so you can get the maximum health benefits. Choose a variety of different-colored whole foods throughout the day and week. The more naturally occurring colors on your plate at each meal or snack, the better!
