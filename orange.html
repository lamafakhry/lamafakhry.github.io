Eat colorfully!

These bright-colored fruits and vegetables contain zeaxanthin, flavonoids, lycopene, potassium, vitamin C and beta-carotene, which is vitamin A. The nutrients help our bodies in many different ways, from our eyes to our bones: Aids in eye health and reduces the risk of macular degeneration of the eye.
